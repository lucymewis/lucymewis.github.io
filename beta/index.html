About

More about me.

I’m an aspiring production manager and theatre professional.

For my future, I’d like to take on many different types of backstage role. From assistant stage manager to lighting designer. Set designer and, of course, production manager.

I’d like to work in small-scale theatre, producing houses, and I wouldn’t be opposed to a little touring.

Fun facts about me

My favourite show is Dead Dog in a Suitcase, and my favourite tool? The kimble gun.
